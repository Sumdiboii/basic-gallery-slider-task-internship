F8 Tributo

The Ferrari F8 Tributo is a stunning mid-engine supercar that celebrates Ferrari's iconic V8 engine. With 710 horsepower and exceptional handling, it combines blistering speed with striking Italian design, offering a thrilling driving experience and a refined, aerodynamic silhouette.

Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is a hypercar icon, renowned for its extraordinary power and luxury. Powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbo W16 engine producing 1,479 horsepower, the Chiron can achieve mind-boggling speeds while enveloping its passengers in opulence, with a design that combines elegance and aerodynamic efficiency.

Miura

The Lamborghini Miura is a legendary Italian sports car produced from 1966 to 1973. Designed by Marcello Gandini, its mid-engine layout and sleek, wedge-shaped body made it a groundbreaking car for its time. The Miura's powerful V12 engine and exceptional performance solidified its status as one of the most desirable and iconic sports cars ever created.

McLaren 765LT

The McLaren 765LT is a lightweight, track-focused supercar that pushes the boundaries of performance. Equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing 755 horsepower, it delivers lightning-fast acceleration, razor-sharp handling, and an aggressive, aerodynamic design that emphasizes speed and agility.

Huayra R

The Pagani Huayra R is a track-only masterpiece, designed to deliver the ultimate driving experience. With a naturally aspirated V12 engine producing 850 horsepower, the Huayra R offers a raw, visceral driving experience, complemented by Pagani's trademark attention to detail and craftsmanship in both design and engineering.
